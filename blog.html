    Creative Commons

    A creative commons license, abbreviated as "CC", is what enables free distribution of work that is copyright-protected. Copyright is the legal right of an owner to whatever they produce. CCs are used when an author wants to share their work without it being stolen or changed. It provides flexibility for the author to choose whether they want to allow commercial distribution or non-commercial, and overall specify the conditions.
