    Principle 1: Alignment- All the text is aligned in the center of the side banner, overlaying the background art.

    Principle 2: Hierarchy- The title text is big and at the top, while the supporting and descriptive text is smaller and at the bottom.

    Principle 3: Color optimization- The color green is used to coincide with the default colors of the chess board, while the text is white in tandem with board pieces.
