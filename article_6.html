    The top ranked esports team for Chess as of right now is the Saint Louis Arch Bishops. What makes this team good is the number of Grandmasters on the team, such as Anna Sharevich and Conrad Holt. What this team does well is hold themselves in such high esteem and take pride in both their players and victory. A flaw this team has is the risky moves that some members such as Matthew Larson and Spencer Finegold play, as well as Holt only winning a tied game due to his earlier preparation with Wesley So.
